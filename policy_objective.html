Policy Objectives

  • To establish an Incubation Center.
  • To encourage students to choose entrepreneurship as their careers.
  • To offer students from rural regions of India, training in business opportunity identification in their local areas.
  • To equip students with the necessary skills for managing their business enterprise.
  • To motivate students to convert their Detailed Project Report (DPRs) into viable B-plans.
  • To create a common virtual platform and ask institutions to submit students projects on this platform to make the project nationwide.
  • To inculcate social responsive behaviors among students to aspiring to launch startups.
  • To orient students as to how they can conceptualize social business startups that will address social issues.
  • To provide handholding support to students for launching their startups during the entire course of their study.
  • To promote awareness about intellectual property rights (IPR) protection and provide legal support and Fast-tracking patent examination at lower cost.
  • To facilitate incubate to enable them to gain benefits from GOI’s ‘Startup India’ programme.
