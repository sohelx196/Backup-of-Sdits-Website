VISION

The Innovation & Startup Policy of the Institute is a guiding framework to envision an educational system oriented towards startup & entrepreneurship opportunities for students & faculties.
In order to achieve the and attitudinal shift & to ensure that "Innovations and startup" culture is the primary fulcrum of higher education system a policy framework and guidelines are the need of this hour. The guidelines will help the faculty, staff & students to participate in innovation and entrepreneurship related activities, thus encouraging students and faculty to consider startups & entrepreneurship as a career option.
